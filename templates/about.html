#1 A Place To Find A Perfect Solution

We are here to guide the farmers with best solutions.

Crop Recommendation – The best crop to grow in your farm according to your location and weather conditions.

Fertilizer Recommendation – Which fertilizers to use according to the nutrients level (NPK values) of your crop.

Disease Detection – Diseases caught by your crops (if any) and how to cure that disease, else classify them as healthy.

Knowledge Section – Innovative ideas and tips for farming i.e Blogs for users which will be added through the admin panel. You can give your suggestions in contact section we will add them.

Farming is one of the major sectors that influences a country’s economic growth. In country like India, majority of the population is dependent on agriculture for their livelihood. Many new technologies, such as Machine Learning and Deep Learning, are being implemented into agriculture so that it is easier for farmers to grow and maximize their yield.

“Farmers are vulnerable to insect attacks and crops are susceptible to diseases” if they are not controlled, depending upon the situation, and climatic condition. 80-90% crop losses are possible if plant protection is not done properly. To counter this need farmers need to be educated on the use of plant protection mechanisms. The basic objective is to give exposure to the Indian farmers on modern methods, to boost agriculture , helping the new farmers by acting as a mentor and Improving quality of rural life.

India has enough food; does it have too many people working in agriculture? The pressure on land is an outcome of policy, which condemns most people to marginal farming. India needs a different set of solutions for agriculture and for those working the land.

The main objective is to bring awareness to farmers about the importance of implementing knowledge and best practices to improve the production system to yield more food. With Meadow , farmers can find best farm practices in the form of tasks which guide them chronologically what to do during a growing season.
